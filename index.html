About Us

Play is how children learn.
We make it unforgettable.

Legacy Sand Adventures was born from a simple belief: children deserve experiences that spark their imagination, engage their senses, and create lasting family memories. We built a mobile sand play experience that brings that joy directly to families, schools, and communities across Northern Virginia.

We're proud to be a local, family-owned business. Every event is an opportunity to make a child's day — and to give parents and organizers a stress-free, memorable experience they'll want to book again and again.

We bring the fun to you! We proudly provide mobile play experiences throughout:

  • Northern Virginia
  • Washington, DC
  • Montgomery County, Maryland
  • Prince George's County, Maryland

Outside our standard service area? Contact us! We may be able to accommodate your event for an additional travel fee.
